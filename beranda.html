E-commerce Anda Dengan Haistar

Haistar adalah perusahaan solusi e-logistic yang memiliki tim manajemen berpengalaman di industri,
gudang di lima kota, manajemen sistem gudang yang terintegrasi, dan infrastruktur serta proses bisnis yang sesuai dengan standar industri.

Haistar bertujuan menjadi rekan yang memudahkan Anda dalam memperbesar skala bisnis, apapun ukurannya. Baik Anda baru memulai, sedang bertumbuh, atau justru bisnis Anda sudah terlalu besar untuk masih ditangani sendiri, kami dapat menyesuaikan solusi kami dengan kebutuhan spesifik Anda.

About Us

Started in April 2018, Haistar aims to help business owners to scale up their businesses by eliminating barriers such as space, manpower, and system issues translated through industry-standard business process and integrated Warehouse Management System.

Haistar focuses on various size of business starting from social media online shop, marketplace merchant or seller, e-commerce, and brand oﬃcial online store who wants to scale up their operation and manage larger order volume.

Haistar will manage clients' operations so that client can focus on expanding their business demand and product development.

Vision

To become your preferred partner in scaling up and accelerating your business

Mission

  Providing business owners with easy to use, hassle-free integrated system

  Providing business owners with industry standard infrastructures and business process

  Providing business owners reasonable investment to scale up their business upon entering and growing their e-commerce presence

  Giving business owners tools to monitor their operational Visibility
